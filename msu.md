# Minecraft System of Units (MSU)

## Base units

| Dimension | Symbol | Name           | Conversion            | Definition                                                                |
|-----------|--------|----------------|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| L         | bl     | block          | 1 m                   | Length of the edge of a Block of Gold                                     |
| T         | t      | tick           | 1/20 s                | 1/200 of the smelting time of Raw Gold into Gold Ingot in a furnace       |
| M         | gd     | golder         | 19 320 kg             | Mass of 1 Block of Gold                                                   |
| Θ         | f      | furn           | ... K                 | Temperature of a furnace while smelting Raw Gold into Gold Ingot in 200 t |
| Q         | rl     | redstone level | ... C                 | 1/15 of the redstone level produced by a Block of Redstone                |
| N         | px     | pixel          | 23.94719750637857 mol | Amount of substance in 1/4096 a Block of Gold                             |
| J         | ll     | light level    | ... cd                | 1/15 of the light emitted by a Glowstone block                            |
